## 📖 My Story

I didn't start in a corporate boardroom. I started in retail—learning the fundamentals of customer service, operations, and what it truly means to deliver excellence. That foundation shaped everything.

As a **Store Manager at Pioneer Power Solutions**, I discovered the power of customer engagement and strategic thinking. Managing operations, driving revenue through consultative selling, and training teams taught me that success is built on relationships and understanding people.

Then came my pivot into **customer experience operations at Wakefit**, where I managed the entire service delivery lifecycle. I didn't just resolve tickets—I reimagined processes. I coordinated cross-functional teams, conducted root cause analysis, and helped achieve significant improvements in customer satisfaction. That's when I realized my true passion: **turning insight into action through CRM excellence and customer-centric strategies**.

Today, as **Co-Founder & Business Development Manager at Your-Creator**, I'm helping SMEs grow through strategic digital solutions—websites, SEO, content creation, and brand storytelling. Combined with my **MBA from University of Northampton** specializing in CRM and customer experience, I blend data-driven strategy with practical execution.

Every customer interaction is an opportunity to improve processes, enhance experiences, and build sustainable business growth.
